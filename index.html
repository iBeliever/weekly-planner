Weekly Planner is a simple and minimalistic planner focused on helping you plan you week, without any extra clutter or unnecessary features.

Features

  • A task list for each day of the week
  • A task list for tasks without a due date
  • Easily add tasks to a particular day of the week
  • Move tasks between days
  • Uncompleted tasks from the last week end up in the Uncategorized list
  • Times are automatically highlighted for quick recognition
